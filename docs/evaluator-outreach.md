## Objective

Get 5 qualitative reviews from founders, contractors, or agencies who have touched an AI-built prototype.

## Screening Prompt

```text
Have you recently built or reviewed a Lovable/Bolt/Replit/Cursor/v0 prototype that needed to become launch- or contractor-ready?
```

## Founder DM Draft

```text
I built a small tool called SpecRelay for founders using AI app builders.

It turns a prototype description into a launch readiness score, risk list, QA checklist, and developer handoff brief.

Could you try one sample packet and tell me whether this would save time before a contractor or pilot review?

https://willsuo-github.github.io/specrelay/
```

## Contractor DM Draft

```text
Quick question: when a founder sends you an AI-built prototype, what information is usually missing before you can scope the next engineering pass?

I made SpecRelay to turn that messy prototype state into a QA checklist and handoff packet:

https://willsuo-github.github.io/specrelay/

The useful feedback would be whether the packet is concrete enough for a first scoping call.
```

## Agency Intake Draft

```text
I am testing SpecRelay as a lightweight intake tool for AI-built app prototypes.

The output is a readiness score, risk list, QA checklist, and engineering brief that a founder can send before an agency/contractor call.

Would this fit your intake process, or is it missing something essential?

https://willsuo-github.github.io/specrelay/
```

## Guardrails

- Do not send any message automatically from a company or personal account without action-time confirmation.
- If posting publicly, verify composer text, URL, and visual preview before publishing.
- Count only direct replies or observed packet use as evidence; do not infer traction from page availability.
- For X, start with specific public replies to recent relevant posts before sending URL-heavy DMs.
