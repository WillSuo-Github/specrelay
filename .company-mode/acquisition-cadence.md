## Cadence Options

| Stage | Default posture | Typical moves |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `pre-product` | research and first evidence source | market map, pain signals, competitor scan, target segment |
| `pre-launch` | prepare first external touch | landing/support page, App Store metadata, channel selection, launch assets |
| `no-traction` | aggressive zero-cost signal creation | posts, replies, channel tests, App Store keyword iteration, target-customer pool |
| `weak-traction` | double down or diagnose | activation fixes, follow-up posts, feedback synthesis, conversion tests |
| `monetization-search` | prove willingness to pay | price/package test, paid offer copy, purchase path, direct user research |
| `scaling` | repeat what works | channel cadence, lifecycle messaging, review mining, store optimization |
