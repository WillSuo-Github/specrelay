# CEO After-Action Review

Run this before ending every active company operation, after role-specific execution and before the final stop-check and next-wake decision.

## CEO Status Report

Produce a concise report with:

- Current stage and active product state
- Evidence gathered, evidence missing, and evidence quality
- Work completed in the current run
- Active blockers, waits, or gates
- Active role conclusions
- Next company decision and next wake outcome

## CEO Company Review

Product/CEO must inspect the whole company:

- Product strategy, roadmap, UX, engineering quality, reliability, distribution, positioning, pricing readiness, support, research, release operations, analytics, automation, and company rules
- Whether the company is passively waiting while internal work exists
- Whether durable state files are stale, incomplete, or contradictory
- Whether the active product still fits this one-product company instance
- Whether a discovered improvement is instance-only, kernel, template, migration, or hard-gated

## Authority

Execute non-hard-gated improvements without asking user approval. If the improvement is reusable across companies, update the shared kernel or starter kit. If the improvement is gated, complete reversible prep work and stop only at that gate.
